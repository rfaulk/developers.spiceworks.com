What's a SpiceButton?

SpiceButtons are Spiceworks’ version of simple, embeddable social media buttons… specifically meant for IT. Just grab a line or two of code and you’re off and running… social IT style.

Follow

Wanna make sure IT pros to engage with you, where they work, when they're working?

Use the 'Follow' social button to:

  • Have IT pros follow your Spiceworks Vendor Page with one click
  • Stream posts from your Vendor Page to their Activity Stream
  • Have some fun—IT doesn’t have to be boring!

Kids love to play games… none more universal than “Follow the Leader.” IT pros were kids at one time (and many still are at heart), so bring back the joy of youth and let them Follow you.

Follow is a simple way for IT pros to learn more about your business… where and when they work. When IT pros follow your Vendor Page, content from your page will stream to their Activity Feed. As these IT pros engage with your Page, their social connections are notified, and can draw new followers.

Login with Spiceworks

Make IT easy! Let IT pros use their Spiceworks IDs on your site.

Use the 'Login with Spiceworks' button to:

  • Enable IT pros to sign on or register on your site with their Spiceworks ID
  • Simplify and socialize their experience on your site
  • Help reduce username and password proliferation along the way

Animal shelters ask for us to help control the pet population. We say help control username and password proliferation. Use Login with Spiceworks, and make IT easier (and more social) along the way.

Login with Spiceworks is a simple way for IT pros to sign-in or join your login system, with their 'IT' social profile. IT just makes sense! Use the profile they use while doing their jobs. When IT pros log into your site with their Spiceworks IDs they bring IT with them… to you.
